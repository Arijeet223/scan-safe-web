Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly will you respond to my inquiry?

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24-48 hours during business days. For urgent matters related to active scams, we prioritize responses.

I've been scammed. Can you help me recover my money?

While we can't directly recover funds, we can guide you through the reporting process with your bank and law enforcement. The sooner you act, the better your chances of recovery.

Do you offer in-person training for organizations?

Yes, we provide customized security training sessions for businesses, schools, and community groups. Contact us through the form above to discuss your specific needs.

How can I support Scan Safe's mission?

You can support us by spreading awareness, reporting scams you encounter, volunteering for our community programs, or partnering with us if you represent an organization.
