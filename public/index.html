How to Stay Safe

Verify the Source

Only scan QR codes from trusted sources. Be wary of codes in public places or unsolicited emails.

Check URLs Before Proceeding

Preview the URL before opening it. Look for misspellings or suspicious domains.

Use Security Software

Keep your device protected with updated security software that can detect threats.
