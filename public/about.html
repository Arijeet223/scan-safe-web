About Scan Safe

Our mission to protect users from QR code scams

Our Story

Scan Safe was launched in 2023 in response to the alarming rise in QR code scams across India. What began as a small initiative to educate friends and family quickly grew into a comprehensive platform dedicated to protecting all users from the growing threat of QR code-based fraud.

Our team of cybersecurity experts, consumer advocates, and digital education specialists work tirelessly to track emerging scam techniques, analyze trends, and develop actionable guidance for everyday users.

Our Mission

To empower every Indian with the knowledge and tools needed to safely navigate the digital payment landscape and protect themselves from QR code scams.

Our Values

Education First

We believe that knowledge is the most powerful defense against scams. We're committed to making cybersecurity education accessible to everyone.

Consumer Advocacy

We advocate for stronger consumer protections and work with financial institutions to improve security measures and recovery options.

Inclusivity

We're dedicated to reaching users of all backgrounds, ages, and technical abilities across both urban and rural India.

Proactive Protection

We don't just respond to existing scams – we actively work to identify emerging threats before they become widespread.
